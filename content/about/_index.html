The Autonomous Robotics Club of Purdue maintains a variety of projects which explore challenging problems in the space of robotics. In the past, we have worked on anything from soccer-playing cars to self-organizing chess pieces. If you are interested in joining, you should check here.

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{< blocks/section >}}

Projects


Projects are pitched by students at the start of each semester. Following all pitches, the leadership team will decide which are worth pursuing. Projects often seek collaboration with research professors on campus to ensure they have proper resources and guidance. If you are interested in learning on ongoing / past projects, they are all documented on the wiki.

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}

The Leadership Team


The leadership team is responsible for overseeing projects and ensuring they have all the necessary resources. Students are selected for roles at the end of each semester.
